Whistler Bike Park, BC.

This is one of my favourite Bike parks I like to visit. There are a ton of trails for all abilities here. My top 3 intermediate trails here are B-Line, Ho Chi Min and Heart of Darkness

Mount Seymour trails, BC.

On the North Shore of Vancouver, this tends to be the quiter mountain to go ride on. It is Packed with fun trails and less crowded. I love riding here and my favourite trail is called John Deere.
